About Us

Our Story

Afri-Ag SL Limited grew from a small Vegetable garden in a small piece of land and now growing to new heights and markets. We are specialized in the cultivation and processing of essential cereals, tubers. vegetables and grains. We cultivate, process, package and distribute essential food stuffs within Sierra Leone and its neighbouring countries (Guinea and Liberia). Products are always available round the clock, year in year out. All you need to do is place your order and necessary processes would be followed to make sure your product is delivered. you can place your order by filling out the form below. We are also into fish farming and animal husbandry which are components of the company that are on the development stage to be launched soon.
