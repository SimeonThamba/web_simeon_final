Our Products

Vegetable Cultivation and Processing

We grow, process and sell variety of vegetables within Sierra Leone for now. In the vegetables section, our aim is to become the number one producer of fresh organic vegetables that would be consumed locally and in neighbouring countries such as Guinea and Liberia.

Fish Farming

As part of our operations, we run a fish farm where we raise and sell variety of fishes such as the cat fish and tilapia in a moderate scale for now with plans to expand to reach more markets.

Cassava Cultivation and Processing

Another part of our operations focuses on the cultivation of cassave which we later process after maturing into garie and cassave flour. We aim at providing an alternative to the traditionl flour that is becoming more than expensive by the day.
