About Us

We are a start up in the agri-business sector specialized in the cultivation and processing of essential vegetables, tubers, cereals and grains, and in addition fish farming and animal husbandary. We cultivate, process, package and distribute essential vegetables and cereals within Sierra Leone and its neighbouring countries (Guinea and Liberia). Products are always available round the clock, year in year out. All you need to do is place your order and necessary processes would be followed to make sure your product is delivered. You can place your order by filling out the form on the contact page, kindly click the link to the contact page. We are also into fish farming and animal husbandry which are components of the company that are on the development stage to be launched soon.

This is an established agribusiness focused on the cultivation and processing of essential cereals, grains and vegetables. Established in 2021 with the main focus of contributing to the food chain of Sierra Leone and by extension Africa.
